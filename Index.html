About Me

As a 5th-semester fashion design student, I am dedicated to transforming my creative vision into reality. My academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation in Adobe Creative Suite, including Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. Additionally, I am skilled in using Figma and Canva, which have been instrumental in developing a diverse range of design projects. Whether it's crafting logos, designing marketing materials, or creating compelling social media graphics, I bring a keen eye for detail and a passion for aesthetics to every task.

My portfolio showcases a blend of innovative concepts and practical design solutions, reflecting my commitment to excellence and my drive to push the boundaries of traditional fashion design.

What truly excites me is the potential of CLO 3D software. This cutting-edge tool offers a new dimension to fashion design by allowing me to visualize and simulate garments in a virtual environment. The ability to see how fabrics move and interact in 3D enhances my design process, enabling me to make more informed decisions and deliver designs that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. As I continue to explore and master this technology, I look forward to integrating it into my design practice to create groundbreaking fashion pieces that resonate with modern audiences.
